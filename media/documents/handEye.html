body language in business

What is a body Language ?

Body language is a type of a nonverbal communication in which physical behaviors,
as opposed to words, are used to express or convey the information.
Such behavior includes facial expressions, body posture,
gestures, eye movement, touch and the use of space. Body language exists in both animals and humans
, but this article focuses on interpretations of human body language. It is also known as kinesics.

Why body Language is important

If we are trying to make a good impression – both socially and in business – we often smile and hold contact the other person’s eyes as we shake their hand.
The difficulty is that we give off thousands of other unconscious signals through our body language that other people will ‘read’ instantly and instinctively.
